Giving HR leadership a live view of attrition and hiring

manual reporting
PROBLEM

APPROACH

Partnered with stakeholders to define the KPIs that mattered (attrition rate, hiring trends, overtime impact, department performance), sourced data from HRMS and payroll, then built the pipeline end to end: SQL extraction and validation, cleaning in Power Query, a star-schema model, and advanced DAX measures.

OUTCOME
