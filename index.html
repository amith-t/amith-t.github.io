financial services · python · sql · power bi

Finding the customers about to leave, before they left

+8%
renewal lift
PROBLEM

Renewals were slipping quarter over quarter and nobody could say which customers were at risk until the cancellation came in.

APPROACH

Built monthly cohorts from servicing data in SQL, then a logistic regression in Python on payment behavior, support contacts, and product mix. Surfaced the three highest-risk segments in a Power BI view the retention team checks weekly.

OUTCOME

The targeted retention campaign run against those segments lifted renewals by 8%. The risk view is now part of the team's standing weekly review.

personal project · time series · tableau public

Forecasting Divvy bike demand, station by station

12 mo
ride history modeled
PROBLEM

Chicago's bike-share stations regularly run empty or full at the wrong times. When does each station need rebalancing?

APPROACH

Modeled a year of public Divvy ride data with SARIMA and Prophet to forecast station-level demand by hour and weekday, accounting for seasonality and weather patterns.

OUTCOME

A Tableau Public story showing which stations need morning vs. evening rebalancing, with the forecast accuracy honestly reported, including where the models fall apart.
